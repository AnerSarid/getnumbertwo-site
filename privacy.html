Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your Data Stays Yours

Number Two stores all your data locally on your device. We never sell your health information to anyone.

Local Storage

All bowel movement logs, statistics, and settings are stored on your device using a local database. The app works fully offline without an account.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, your logs are encrypted in transit and synced to our cloud provider (Supabase) so you can restore them on a new device. You can delete all cloud data at any time from Settings.

Leaderboard (Optional)

If you opt in to the Throne Room leaderboard, an anonymous username and your weekly scores are stored in the cloud. No health details are shared — only aggregate scores.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, which receives purchase receipts and a device identifier to verify entitlements. RevenueCat does not receive your health data.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or ad tracking services. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no telemetry.

Data Export & Deletion

You can export your data as CSV or PDF at any time. To delete all cloud data, use the Delete Account option in Settings. Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app.
